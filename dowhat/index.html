For this assignment, I created a set of instructions which was given to another student to see if she got the same results I did.

Instructions:
Fold a regular A4 paper in half horizontally. Following the crease created, draw a straight line with a pencil. Over the line, draw 10 dots
with equal distance between each other. Starting from the first dot and stretching upwards, draw zig-zag lines all the way through the dots. Then,
draw another zig-zag lines through the dots starting from the first dot and stretching downwards. Then, color in the triangles created between
intersections of the two zig-zag lines.
